## Art-direction notes
- **Locked kit honored:** Accent #D81E5B, Display = Melodrama (Fontshare), Body = Satoshi (Fontshare),
  layout = fashion-editorial poster grid / lookbook plates, mood = fashion-editorial.
- **Signature interaction (mine alone this sprint):** hover-reveal lookbook plates. Each plate opens on a
  quiet "rack / on-the-hanger" state and on hover (tap on mobile) crossfades to a styled, worn LOOK while a
  caption plate slides up from the bottom. The two states are deliberately unmistakable at a glance
  (per the 06-12 lesson: the reveal only lands if both states read instantly).
- **Palette:** warm bone paper (#F6F1EA) ground, ink (#16110F) type, single magenta accent #D81E5B used
  sparingly (rules, caption bars, the call CTA). Editorial, gallery-quiet, lets garments carry color.
- **Type personality:** Melodrama for the big romantic display headings, Satoshi for crisp UI/body.
- **Live status pill:** computed in America/New_York from 9:30 AM–7 PM daily. 12-hour time everywhere.
- **Maps:** keyless Ramos-pattern iframe centered on 167 Main St, City of Orange NJ.
- **No em dashes** anywhere in visible copy.
